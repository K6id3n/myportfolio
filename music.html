My music Career

I have been interested in music since 5th grade, I started by playing the saxophone but eventually, I changed my focus to percussion.

I have played in the percussion section for 6 years, playing in my high school ensembles like the Wind Symphony, Marching band, and Indoor Percussion

I also hope to one day be able to play in the Francisco symphony and have marched in the Blue Devils Drum and Bugle corp.
